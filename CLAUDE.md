## Design Context

### Users
Primary audience is peers, recruiters, collaborators, and judges reviewing Vincent's product work and engineering quality. They typically browse quickly on mobile first, then inspect details on desktop. The main job-to-be-done is quickly understanding project quality, then diving into deeper case-study style details.

### Brand Personality
Confident, premium, human. The interface should feel polished and high-signal, with an Apple-inspired balance of clarity and craft. Emotionally, the site should convey trust, momentum, and design maturity.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light-first editorial product showcase with soft gradients, restrained depth, and deliberate typography hierarchy. Motion should be smooth and purposeful, not flashy, using natural easing and calm transitions. Avoid generic card spam, heavy neon palettes, and over-decorated effects.

### Design Principles
1. Clarity first: every section should communicate value within seconds.
2. Premium restraint: use subtle depth, spacing rhythm, and high-quality typography instead of visual noise.
3. Purposeful motion: animations should explain hierarchy and state, always respecting reduced-motion preferences.
4. Mobile integrity: do not hide critical content on small screens; adapt layouts while preserving meaning.
5. Content-driven architecture: structured JSON metadata with markdown-backed long-form content for easy publishing and maintenance.
