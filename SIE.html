Meet Our Founder

Vincent Chiang

Vincent, a WWDC Swift Student Challenge winner, is a passionate coder dedicated to using technology to solve real-world problems. During high school, he self-taught over five programming languages and led his teams to excel in various competitions, transforming the Robotics Club at Neihu High School into a thriving organization.

Now an Application Engineer at JetFi Mobile, Vincent is gaining invaluable experience building apps from the ground up while honing his skills in business and team management. As the founder of S.I.E., he combines technological innovation with social impact, leading projects like the Digital Manual platform and other groundbreaking solutions.

Humble yet ambitious, Vincent continues to grow as a developer and leader, committed to creating meaningful change through technology.
