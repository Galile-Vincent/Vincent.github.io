VCook

VCook is an intelligent cooking companion designed to make preparing meals simple, fun, and personalized. Using on-device AI, VCook generates customized recipes based on your available ingredients, health data, exercise activity and dietary preferences. Whether you're missing a key ingredient or want a healthier option, VCook offers smart substitute suggestions and step-by-step guidance, complete with timers and tips. With voice control and gesture-based navigation, you can confidently cook without having to touch your device, even with messy hands. Developed by Taiwanese iOS engineer Vincet Chiang, VCook won the First Prize at the 2025 Mobile Application Innovation Contest (MAIC) for its innovative use of AI to transform everyday cooking.
